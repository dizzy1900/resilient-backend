Methodology

Projections are derived from Resilient's physics-informed climate model ensemble, incorporating CMIP6 downscaled scenarios (SSP2-4.5 and SSP5-8.5), asset-level exposure data, and Monte Carlo simulation across 10,000 stochastic pathways. Net Present Value calculations apply a risk-adjusted discount rate reflecting sovereign risk, sector volatility, and climate beta. Default probability estimates are calibrated against a global peer set of comparable agricultural and infrastructure assets. Credit ratings represent Resilient's proprietary Climate Credit Assessment and are not ratings issued by any regulated credit rating agency. All figures represent median estimates; 90th-percentile stress values are available upon request.
